Bubble Sort->Bubble Sort repeatedly compares adjacent elements and swaps them if they are in the wrong order. After each pass, the largest element "bubbles" to the end of the list.

Insertion Sort->Insertion Sort builds the sorted portion of the array one element at a time by inserting each new element into its correct position.

Quick Sort->Quick Sort is a divide-and-conquer algorithm that selects a pivot element, places it in its correct position, and recursively sorts the left and right subarrays.
